Brake Lever
contributer - mikethemitch - www.benelliforum.com
The Brembo lever from an Aprilia RSV Mille or Ducati 748/916 will fit straight on the Tornado.

contributer - Paul Smith, 13 Jan 2007 - www.benelliforum.com- there's some that do & some that don't ...
Aprillia RSV Mille/r 99 to 03 will work
Aprillia Tuono/r 03 to 07 will work
Aprillia Falco 00 to 04 will work
Ducati 748 99-02 will work
Ducati 996/998 all models will work
Ducati 750SS 99-02 will work
Ducati 900ss /1000ss 98-06 will work
Ducati M900 /M1000 00-05 will work
Ducati MS4 /MS4R 01-06 will work
 Ducati 748 97 to 98 will not work
Ducati 749 03 to 06 will not work
Ducati 916 will not work
Ducati 999/1098 will not work
Aprillia RSV Mille/r 04 to 07 will not work.
 You can check for other models that will work or not at ASV Inventions. The Part number that works is BRC555

Clutch Lever
contributers - mikethemitch & Dino - www.benelliforum.com
Non-Adjustable Clutch Lever : The clutch lever from an Aprilia RS125/250 will fit the Tornado.
Adjustable Clutch Lever : The Tornado clutch lever is made by a company called Domino. A company in Italy called Red Racing Parts do the lever for 23.91 Euros which works out at about GPB15.99. You have to add VAT and postage but it still only works out at about GBP28.50
Link to Red Racing Parts Look down left hand menu for "Clutch", under clutch select "Wire Controls".
On the right hand page select "Road / Racing Applications".
The top right lever will be the one - part #111.61.32

Gear Lever
contributer - speedster46 (24 May 2012) - www.benelliforum.com
"anyone who likes changing gear 1 up 5 down do away with the standard gear linkage and fit a lever straight off the shaft much more feel and helps with finding neutral I used a lever off a motorcross bike part number GPF401 YamahaYZ 97/12 GBP17.00"
(This is how Pete Goddard's race bike was set up)
