Fitting an Alarm
(updated 11 June 2006)

The loom has a plug inside the right hand side of the front upper fairing, that the Gemel (META) E587T will plug into, with no additional wiring....(Mikethmitch, 21 Sep 2005)
I (laurencewhite, 21 Sep 2005) also fitted an additional unit that sends a message to my Mobile phone if the bike is disturbed, which gets round the limited range of a Pager unit. You can also trace where the bike is on the Web, if it does happen to get nicked. How cool is that?
