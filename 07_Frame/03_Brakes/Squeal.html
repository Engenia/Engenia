Brake Squeal (updated 02 Dec 2007)

Posted by colintornado - Benelliforum - 2 Nov 07
My rear brake did the squealing. I eventually found it was a sticky piston. One piston would move a lot easier than the other. I put a spanner between the pads and pumped the pistons both out enough to polish them (the pistons). Since then my squeal has gone !
